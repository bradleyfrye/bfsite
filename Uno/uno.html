UNO - How to Play

- Starting the game: The dealer shuffles the deck and deals 7 cards to each player. The dealer then draws one card from the draw pile and places it in the discard pile. Play then starts with the player after the dealer. Any cards with special effects are applied to the next player, and the dealer chooses a color in the case of a Wild card. (Note: in this game, you are always the dealer and these steps are taken automatically.)

- Playing the game: On a player's turn, the player may either play a card from their hand, or pass. To play a card, it must either match the color of the card on the discard pile (or the color chosen by someone playing a wild), or match the value of the top card of the discard pile, i.e. its number or letter(s). Wild cards can be played at any time, and the player must select a color before the next player takes their turn. If the player does not have any valid cards to play, they must pass. When passing, a player draws a card from the deck. If this card is playable, the player may play this card, but they can't play any other cards from their hand at this point. If the card is not playable, or the player does not wish to play it, they can pass again to end their turn.

- Special cards: Certain cards have special effects when played. They are:
     Draw 2 (D2) - The next player must draw 2 cards, and their turn is skipped.
     Skip (S) - The next player's turn is skipped.
     Reverse (R) - The order of play is reversed. If there are only 2 players, this just acts like a Skip.
     Wild (W) - Can be played at any time. The player who used this card selects a new color.
     Wild Draw 4 (W4) - Same as Wild, but the next player must draw 4 cards, and their turn is skipped.

- Winning the game: The first player to play all of their cards is the winner. (Note: the saying of "UNO" has not been implemented. Considering putting in a button that you have to click for this or something.)
