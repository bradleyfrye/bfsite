What does this program do?

This program is just a simple online list of items, organized by groups of people into houses. Users on this website can log in, and open a house that they're a member of, where they can view the list and add/remove items from it.

How to use:

Step 0: You need to create a user and log in to use this program. Creating a user on this site is easy, immediate, and secure - click here to register and click here to log in.

Step 1: Visiting this page as a logged in user, you'll see house icons showing any houses you're a member of, a link to join a house, and a link to create a house. Click the create link if you'd like to make a new list for a group of people (or just for yourself.) On the create page, just specify a name and click Submit, and the house is made.

Step 2: If you'd like to add yourself to a house someone else created, click the join link. Enter the name of the house you'd like to join (case doesn't matter), click submit, and you'll be added to the house.

Step 3: To view a list, click on the corresponding house icon on the main page. From here, you can add items by entering a quantity and item description, and click Add Item or press Enter. You can remove items from the list by clicking the X button in the right column.
